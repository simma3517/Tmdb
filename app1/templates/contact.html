Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and platform.

How do I create an account? -
Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website. Fill in your details, verify your email address, and you're all set to explore our vast collection of movies and TV shows.
How can I contribute to the database? +
We welcome contributions from our community! Once you have an account, you can suggest new titles, edit existing information, add images, and write reviews. All contributions are reviewed by our moderation team before being published.
Is TMDB available as a mobile app? +
Yes! We have apps available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download them from the App Store or Google Play Store. Our apps offer the same great features as our website, optimized for mobile use.
How do I access the TMDB API? +
Our API is available for developers who want to integrate our movie and TV show data into their applications. To get started, create a developer account, request an API key, and check out our comprehensive documentation for implementation guidelines.
How accurate is your data? +
We strive for maximum accuracy in our database. Our information comes from official sources and is regularly updated. We also have a dedicated team verifying user contributions and making corrections as needed. If you spot any errors, please report them!
