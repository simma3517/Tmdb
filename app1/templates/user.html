Our Mission

At TMDB, we believe that everyone deserves access to comprehensive, reliable information about the world of film and television. Our mission is to create the most inclusive, accurate, and user-friendly platform for cinephiles and casual viewers alike.

Founded in 2024 by a team of passionate film enthusiasts, TMDB has quickly evolved from a simple hobby project into a comprehensive database trusted by movie fans worldwide. We're dedicated to helping you discover new favorites, keep track of what you've watched, and connect with a community that shares your passion for storytelling.
